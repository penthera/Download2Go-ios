– downloadEngineIsEnteringBackground:pausingAssets:

Called whenever the Engine is about to enter background

- (void)downloadEngineIsEnteringBackground:(NSArray *_Nullable)continuingAssets pausingAssets:(NSArray *_Nullable)pausingAssets

Parameters

continuingAssets

VirtuosoAsset asset
pausingAssets

NSError for indicating the error details

Discussion

Called some period of time after the main application has been put into the background by the User. Depending on application configuration, the Engine will keep the app alive in the background for some period of time in order to continue active downloads. Once the Engine cannot keep the application alive any longer, due to iOS constraints, the Engine will pause downloads and allow the application to be put to sleep. Before going to sleep, this notification is called to allow the Caller to take potential action if downloads were still pending at this point.
